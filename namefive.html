Put the names of students who want to be a leader into the left hand column, then press the Leaders button. It will designate Red Team and Blue Team leaders. When the students have chosen who wants to be on their team (make sure there are an equal number of students on each team), press the New Pair button to start a game (press Rematch! if it's a draw). Then when the student answers the question correctly, Spin their team's wheel to give them points! After that, press the Next Pair button to go on to the next two students. When finished, press the Reset Game button to clear everything for the next class.
